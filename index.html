Graph Theory/Algorithms-GC

Extended bases are what one obtains after the treatment with the two enzymes, so it could be more than one letter, it's what we obtain between two dots, or at the beginning and end of the fragments. A single extended base does not straddle the /

An interior extended base is between / /, hence the name interior. They must be interior within a whole fragment.

Non-single fragments are fragments that have at least more than one extended base.
